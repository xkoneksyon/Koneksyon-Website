Introduction
About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Zanara Labs LLC ("Koneksyon," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use the Koneksyon platform, including the mobile app and website.

By creating an account or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your information as described herein.

This Privacy Policy works alongside our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Section 1
Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Date of birth, gender, location
  • Profile photos and videos
  • Bio, prompts, and cultural preferences
  • Payment information (processed by third-party providers)
  • Messages and communications sent through the platform
  • Identity verification documents

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage patterns, feature interactions, and session data
  • Push notification tokens
  • Log data and error reports

1.3 Information From Third Parties

We may receive information from verification providers, payment processors, app stores (Apple, Google), and other third-party services integrated with the platform.

B-Konekt Data
B-Konekt Profile, Networking, and Business Information

B-Konekt Information You Provide

If you use B-Konekt, we may collect, store, process, and display information you choose to provide for networking, business, creative, collaboration, mentorship, event, or community purposes. This may include your B-Konekt category, headline, services, offer type, industries, collaboration interests, availability, contact preferences, location, photos, and related profile details.

How B-Konekt Information Is Used

We use B-Konekt information to operate the B-Konekt feature, display relevant profiles, support search and filtering, help users discover culture-first networking and collaboration opportunities, support safety and moderation, investigate reports, prevent misuse, enforce our Terms and Community Guidelines, and improve the Koneksyon experience.

B-Konekt Visibility

B-Konekt information may be visible to other users depending on your profile settings, feature availability, moderation status, and how you choose to complete your B-Konekt profile. Other users may see the B-Konekt details you provide, including your category, services, industries, collaboration interests, availability, contact preferences, general location, approved photos, and related profile details.

User Responsibility

You are responsible for the accuracy of B-Konekt information you choose to share. Do not include private, financial, confidential, unsafe, illegal, or misleading information in your B-Konekt profile or communications. We may review, restrict, hide, remove, or preserve B-Konekt information where necessary for safety, moderation, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or enforcement of our Terms.

Section 2
How We Use Your Information
  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide matching, discovery, and messaging features
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To moderate content and enforce our Community Guidelines
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send notifications, updates, and safety alerts
  • To improve the platform using analytics and AI systems
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To respond to support requests
  • To operate the Ambassador Program if applicable
  • To operate B-Konekt networking, business, collaboration, mentorship, event, and community connection features
Section 3
How We Share Information

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information, photos, and content you choose to share are visible to other users on the platform according to your privacy settings.

3.1A With Other Users Through B-Konekt

If you enable or use B-Konekt, the B-Konekt profile information you choose to provide may be visible to other users for networking, business, creative, collaboration, mentorship, event, and community connection purposes. This may include your B-Konekt category, headline, services, industries, collaboration interests, availability, contact preferences, general location, approved photos, and related profile details.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party providers including Supabase (infrastructure), payment processors, verification providers, analytics services, and cloud storage providers. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information to comply with law, respond to legal requests, protect users or the public, or enforce our Terms of Service.

3.4 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Koneksyon does not sell personal information to third parties for advertising purposes.

Section 4
Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored and processed using Supabase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and monitoring to protect your information.

No system is 100% secure. Koneksyon is not liable for unauthorized access, cyberattacks, or data breaches beyond our reasonable control. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

Section 5
Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Correction

You may access and update your profile information at any time through the app settings.

5.2 Account Deletion

You may request account deletion through the app. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Some information may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or safety purposes.

5.3 GDPR Rights (EEA & UK Users)

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of personal data
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to certain processing
  • Withdrawal of consent

5.4 California Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may request access to, deletion of, or correction of their personal information. Contact us at support@koneksyondatingapp.com to exercise these rights.

Section 6
Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy for full details. The mobile app does not use browser cookies but may use device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.

Section 7
Children's Privacy

Koneksyon is strictly for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will remove it and delete associated data promptly.

Koneksyon also maintains public Child Safety Standards that expressly prohibit Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, trafficking of a minor, sexualization of minors, and any conduct that exploits or endangers children.

If we learn that an account, message, profile, image, or other content may involve a minor or child exploitation, we may remove the content, preserve relevant information, suspend or terminate accounts, and report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or another appropriate regional authority as required by law.

Child Safety contact: childsupport@koneksyondatingapp.com

Section 8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Ambassador Program
Ambassador Data

This information is used solely to operate the Ambassador Program, calculate and process commissions, and maintain audit records as required by law.

Interested in becoming an ambassador? Email us at support@koneksyondatingapp.com.
