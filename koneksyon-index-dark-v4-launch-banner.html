Questions

Clear answers build confidence.

Is Koneksyon only for Caribbean people?

No. Koneksyon is Caribbean-inspired and open to everyone. Culture is part of the brand, but the platform welcomes people from every background.

What is B-Konekt?

B-Konekt is the networking side of Koneksyon for founders, creators, professionals, mentors, services, events, and community opportunities.

How is Spark different from a normal dating feed?

Spark focuses on intention, values, language, roots, lifestyle, and compatibility instead of making the whole product feel like a generic slide-left / slide-right feed.
