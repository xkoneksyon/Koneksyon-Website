Introduction
About This Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Koneksyon, operated by Zanara Labs LLC, uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. Koneksyon is primarily a mobile dating and social connection app. The website may use only basic technologies necessary to operate the site, remember preferences, improve performance, and provide a secure experience.

What Are Cookies
Cookies Explained

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, logs, or device identifiers. These technologies help websites remember your preferences and operate correctly across visits.

How We Use Them
Our Use of Cookies

We may use cookies or local storage to:

  • Remember your light or dark mode preference
  • Support basic website functionality
  • Improve page load performance
  • Understand general website usage patterns
  • Prevent abuse and protect against security risks
  • Keep you signed in to account features where applicable

Service Providers
Supabase & Infrastructure

Koneksyon uses Supabase for app infrastructure, authentication, database, and storage. If website features connect to Supabase, related cookies, local storage, or authentication tokens may be used to keep users signed in, secure sessions, and operate account-related features.

These are strictly operational and are never used for advertising or tracking.

No Advertising
No Ad Cookies

Koneksyon does not use third-party advertising cookies on this website. We do not sell personal information for advertising purposes. We do not allow advertising networks to place tracking cookies on our site.

Koneksyon products are ad-free.

Your Choices
Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for first-party vs third-party cookies

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect some website features, including saved theme preferences or future account-related features.
