Introduction
Purpose of These Standards

Koneksyon is a Caribbean-inspired dating and social connection app operated by Zanara Labs LLC. Koneksyon is designed for adults only and is not directed to children.

These Child Safety Standards explain Koneksyon’s rules and enforcement approach for preventing, detecting, reporting, and removing content or conduct involving Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), and child endangerment.

These standards apply together with our Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and Privacy Policy.

Section 1
Zero Tolerance for CSAE and CSAM

Koneksyon and Zanara Labs LLC strictly prohibit Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, trafficking of a minor, sexualization of minors, and any content or conduct that exploits, abuses, endangers, or targets children.

Users may not upload, create, share, request, promote, distribute, possess, or attempt to obtain CSAE or CSAM content through Koneksyon. Users may not use Koneksyon to contact, groom, exploit, threaten, blackmail, manipulate, or endanger a minor.

Any CSAE, CSAM, grooming, sextortion, trafficking of a minor, or child endangerment concern may lead to immediate account removal and reporting to appropriate authorities.

Section 2
Adults-Only Platform

Koneksyon is only for users who are at least eighteen (18) years old. Minors are not allowed to create accounts, use the app, appear in dating profiles, or participate in conversations on the platform.

Users may not misrepresent their age, create an account for a minor, ask a minor to use the app, or use the app to communicate with minors for sexual, romantic, exploitative, or otherwise inappropriate purposes.

Section 3
User Reporting and In-App Feedback

Koneksyon provides in-app reporting and blocking tools so users can report profiles, photos, messages, and behavior that may violate our safety rules. Users can also contact our Trust & Safety team by email.

To report a child safety concern, use the app’s report feature when available, or email our designated Child Safety contact at childsupport@koneksyondatingapp.com.

Reports should include the username, profile details, screenshots if safe and lawful to share, and a short explanation of the concern. Do not forward, upload, or redistribute suspected CSAM.

Section 4
Submit a Child Safety Report Online

Use this secure online form to report suspected Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, a suspected minor on Koneksyon, or any other child safety concern involving the Koneksyon app.

You may also email our designated Child Safety contact directly at childsupport@koneksyondatingapp.com.

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement or emergency services first. Do not upload, forward, or redistribute suspected Child Sexual Abuse Material.

Reports submitted through this form are sent to the Koneksyon Child Safety team for review.

Section 5
Review, Removal, and Enforcement

When Koneksyon receives a child safety report or obtains actual knowledge of suspected CSAE or CSAM, we may review the content, restrict access, remove content, preserve relevant information, suspend or terminate accounts, block devices or identifiers, and take other action needed to protect users and comply with law.

Koneksyon may use a combination of automated tools, user reports, human review, and third-party services to help detect and enforce child safety standards.

Section 6
Compliance With Child Safety Laws

Koneksyon complies with applicable child safety laws and regulations. When we confirm or obtain actual knowledge of CSAM, we may report it to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or another appropriate regional authority, as required by applicable law.

Koneksyon may also cooperate with law enforcement, regulators, child protection organizations, app stores, and other authorized entities when necessary to investigate, prevent, or respond to child exploitation or child endangerment.

Section 7
Designated Child Safety Point of Contact

Designated Child Safety contact for Koneksyon and Zanara Labs LLC:

childsupport@koneksyondatingapp.com

This contact can receive notifications from Google Play or other authorized parties about potential CSAE content found in Koneksyon and can coordinate review, enforcement, preservation, removal, and reporting actions.
