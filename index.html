Trust and safety

A premium brand needs premium protection.

Koneksyon is positioned as an adults-only platform with safety pages and product rules that support verification, reporting, blocking, moderation, privacy, and child safety standards.

18+ platform

Koneksyon is not designed for minors and should remain clearly adults-only across the website and app stores.

Verification-first

Selfie verification and profile requirements help reduce fake accounts and protect the community.

Report and block

Users should always be able to report unsafe behavior, block unwanted contact, and contact support.

Clear policies

Terms, Privacy, Community Guidelines, and Child Safety pages are linked directly from the footer.
