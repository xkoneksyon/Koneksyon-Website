Frequently asked questions

What makes Koneksyon different?

Is Koneksyon only a dating app?

No. Koneksyon includes personal connection, friendship, creators, entrepreneurs, opportunities, events, Circles, K-Letters, Pop & Konekt, and B-Konekt.

Does Koneksyon use swiping?

No. The platform is organized around Intentions, Opportunities, Experiences, requests, and community participation instead of endless swiping.

Who can join Koneksyon?

Koneksyon is Caribbean-inspired and open to everyone who meets the platform requirements and community standards.

What is B-Konekt?

B-Konekt is the professional side of Koneksyon for creators, entrepreneurs, founders, mentors, clients, collaborators, and opportunities.

What is Let’s Meet?

Let’s Meet helps people turn an active conversation into a real plan by proposing a date, time, place, and activity inside the chat.
