KONEKSYON COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

Operated by Zanara Labs LLC — Florida, United States

Trust & Safety: support@koneksyondatingapp.com

INTRODUCTION

Koneksyon was created to celebrate real Caribbean connection across islands, languages, culture, diaspora, and shared identity. The app is meant to feel warm, intentional, and rooted in community — not rushed, fake, or superficial.

Koneksyon is designed around intentional discovery. The platform is built to encourage thoughtful connection, real effort, and more meaningful interaction instead of endless, careless sliding.

These Community Guidelines are incorporated into and form part of the Koneksyon Terms of Service. By using the app, you agree to follow these Guidelines, the Terms of Service, the Privacy Policy, and applicable law.

SECTION 1 — CORE PRINCIPLES

These values shape how members should present themselves and interact with others on Koneksyon.

1.1 Respect Every Member

Koneksyon is built for real connection, not disrespect. Members must treat each other with dignity, kindness, honesty, and maturity in profiles, conversations, and all interactions connected to the app.

1.2 Be Authentic

Use accurate information and real photos that reflect who you are. Fake profiles, impersonation, stolen photos, misleading identity details, catfishing, and other deceptive conduct are not allowed on Koneksyon.

1.3 Protect Safety

Any conduct that threatens another person’s emotional, physical, sexual, digital, or financial safety may lead to restrictions, suspension, or permanent account removal.

1.4 Honor Culture and Diversity

Koneksyon celebrates Caribbean people, diaspora stories, multilingual expression, and cross-island connection. Respect all backgrounds, accents, cultures, beliefs, and traditions.

SECTION 2 — PROHIBITED CONDUCT

The examples below are illustrative and not exhaustive. Similar harmful conduct may also lead to enforcement action.

2.1 Harassment, Hate, and Abuse

Bullying, threats, stalking, intimidation, coercion, discrimination, hate speech, slurs, blackmail, sexual harassment, repeated unwanted contact, and degrading behavior are prohibited.

2.2 Inappropriate or Explicit Content

Do not upload or send explicit sexual content, exploitative nudity, graphic violence, hateful imagery, or content intended to shock, manipulate, or harm others.

2.3 Scams and Financial Solicitation

Do not ask users for money, gifts, investments, loans, account access, crypto transfers, or any item of value. Romance scams, phishing, deceptive promotion, and fraudulent behavior are strictly prohibited.

2.4 Privacy Violations

Do not share private information without permission, including:

Phone numbers, home addresses, or workplace details

Financial information

Private screenshots

Intimate content shared to shame, expose, or pressure someone

2.5 Spam or Platform Manipulation

Mass messaging, repetitive copy-paste outreach, misleading links, bot-like behavior, engagement farming, and attempts to manipulate discovery, trust signals, moderation systems, or connection systems are not allowed.

2.6 Commercial Misuse

Koneksyon is for connection, not unauthorized advertising. You may not use the platform mainly to sell products, recruit, promote unrelated businesses, drive traffic, or run marketing campaigns without authorization.

2.7 Underage Risk and Exploitation

Koneksyon is for adults only. Any attempt to involve minors, misrepresent age, exploit vulnerable users, groom users, or engage in predatory behavior may result in immediate and permanent enforcement action.

2.8 Circumvention of Enforcement

You may not attempt to bypass, evade, or undermine enforcement actions, including by creating new accounts after suspension or termination, using alternate identities, or attempting to regain access through unauthorized means.

SECTION 3 — PLATFORM NOTES

These notes explain how Koneksyon may protect members, enforce standards, and operate the service.

3.1 These Guidelines Are Part of the Terms

These Community Guidelines are incorporated into and form part of Koneksyon’s Terms of Service. Violations of these Guidelines may lead to enforcement under the Terms in addition to any other action allowed by law or policy.

3.2 Content Moderation and Enforcement

Koneksyon and Zanara Labs LLC may, at their sole discretion and with or without notice, remove content, limit features, restrict visibility, suspend accounts, or permanently remove users when reasonably necessary to protect the community, preserve trust, enforce policy, or comply with law.

3.3 No Obligation to Act

Koneksyon does not guarantee that all content or behavior will be reviewed, detected, or acted upon. We are not obligated to monitor all user activity or to take action in every instance. Decisions regarding enforcement are made at our sole discretion.

3.4 No Guarantee of Safety

While Koneksyon implements moderation systems, reporting tools, and safety features, we do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or effectiveness of these systems, and we do not guarantee that all harmful behavior will be prevented.

3.5 Use of Data in Enforcement

Koneksyon may use profile information, reports, moderation history, behavioral signals, automated detection systems, and other available data to investigate, evaluate, and take enforcement actions under these Guidelines and the Terms of Service.

3.6 Premium Features

Premium features are designed to enhance the experience, but they do not guarantee visibility, profile exposure, replies, likes, connections, dates, or any particular outcome on the platform.

3.7 Off-Platform Conduct

Koneksyon may consider behavior that occurs outside the platform, including on other services or in real-world interactions, where such conduct creates credible safety, fraud, or trust concerns for users or the platform. Enforcement decisions may be based on both on-platform and off-platform information.

3.8 Service Availability

Koneksyon is provided subject to availability. Features, limits, design, discovery logic, moderation tools, and subscription benefits may evolve over time as the platform grows.

3.9 Repeat or Serious Violations

Repeated or serious violations may lead to immediate restriction or permanent removal, with or without prior notice, when Koneksyon determines that action is necessary for safety, trust, fraud prevention, or platform integrity.

3.10 These Examples Are Not Exhaustive

The examples in these Guidelines are illustrative and not exhaustive. Similar conduct, evasive behavior, attempts to work around enforcement, or behavior that undermines safety, respect, authenticity, or trust may also result in action.

3.11 Local Laws

All users must comply with applicable local, state, national, and international laws when using Koneksyon. Illegal conduct is prohibited even if not specifically listed in these Guidelines.

SECTION 4 — SAFETY AND REPORTING

These sections explain how members can raise concerns and how the platform may respond.

4.1 Report Harmful Behavior

If you experience or observe conduct that may violate these Guidelines, use the app’s reporting and blocking tools or contact support. Reports help us investigate safety concerns, enforce rules, and protect the community.

4.2 Review and Appeals

Koneksyon may offer limited review or appeal options for certain enforcement decisions. However, we are not obligated to provide appeals or to reverse any enforcement decision. The availability of review does not require Koneksyon or Zanara Labs LLC to change any outcome, and enforcement decisions remain subject to our reasonable discretion.

4.3 Scope of These Guidelines

These Guidelines apply to profiles, photos, bios, prompts, messages, reports, support interactions, and other behavior connected to your use of Koneksyon. In some cases, behavior outside the app may also be considered where it creates credible safety, fraud, or trust concerns for the platform or its users.

4.4 Law Enforcement Cooperation

Koneksyon may cooperate with law enforcement, regulators, or legal authorities in connection with investigations, safety concerns, or legal obligations, including sharing information where required or permitted by law.

SECTION 5 — ENFORCEMENT AND CONSEQUENCES

Depending on the severity of the conduct, Koneksyon may:

Remove content

Restrict profile visibility

Limit features

Suspend messaging

Temporarily suspend access

Permanently remove an account

Serious violations involving scams, impersonation, underage risk, credible threats, exploitation, repeated abusive behavior, privacy abuse, or major trust breaches may result in immediate action, with or without notice.
