Section 1

Introduction and Overview

1.1 Who We Are

Koneksyon is a Caribbean-inspired dating and social connection app created to help people build authentic relationships based on personality, values, culture, and real compatibility — not just appearances. The app is open to everyone. Koneksyon is available in four languages: Haitian Kreyol, French, Spanish, and English. The app is available on the App Store and Google Play Store. The platform is built and operated by Zanara Labs LLC, a Florida limited liability company.

1.2 Purpose of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Koneksyon collects from you, how we collect it, how we use it, how we store it, how long we keep it, when and with whom we share it, and what rights and choices you may have. This policy is written to reflect exactly what our systems actually collect and store, not general approximations. It is designed to be accurate, complete, and legally protective of both users and Zanara Labs LLC.

1.3 Scope

This policy applies to all users of the Koneksyon mobile application on iOS and Android. It covers the full user lifecycle, including:

  • Account creation, authentication, and onboarding
  • Profile setup, editing, and management
  • Identity verification
  • Profile photo uploads and moderation
  • Discovery, sliding, Sparks, and making connections
  • Message requests, messaging, chats, and date proposals
  • Konekt+ and premium features
  • Support requests and moderation interactions
  • Premium subscriptions, credits, and billing
  • Privacy settings and controls
  • Account deletion and privacy requests

1.4 Acceptance

By creating a Koneksyon account and completing onboarding — including checking the mandatory agreement checkbox on the final onboarding step — you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. Your agreement is recorded in our systems with a timestamp, your user ID, the policy version you agreed to, and the platform you used (iOS or Android). This record is permanent and cannot be deleted.

1.5 Relationship to Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and forms part of the Koneksyon Terms of Service. Capitalized terms not defined in this policy have the meaning given in the Terms of Service.

Section 2

Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Authentication Information

When you create an account, we collect and store:

  • Email address — used for authentication, account recovery, and communications
  • Password — stored in encrypted form; never stored in plain text
  • Date of birth — used to confirm you are at least 18 years old
  • Account creation timestamp
  • Account status — active, paused, or deleted
  • Pause reason and timestamp, if applicable
  • Deletion flag, reason, and timestamp, if applicable
  • Last seen timestamp
  • Expo push token — used to deliver in-app push notifications

2.2 Profile Information You Provide

During onboarding and profile editing, we collect: first name, gender, relationship goal, bio, cultural roots and Caribbean or Haitian background, languages spoken including Haitian Kreyol, language mode preference, energy level, music preferences, food preferences, love language, profile completion status, premium and admin status, and verification status.

Some of this information — particularly cultural background, Haitian or Caribbean origin, ethnic identity signals, gender identity, and personal preferences — may be considered sensitive personal information under applicable privacy laws. By voluntarily providing this information, you explicitly consent to its collection and processing as described in this policy.

2.3 Privacy and Visibility Settings

We store your privacy preferences which control how your profile appears to other users:

  • Show age — whether your age is displayed on your profile
  • Show distance — whether your approximate distance is shown in discovery
  • Show online status — whether other users can see when you are active
  • Show read receipts — whether message read receipts are visible
  • Hidden from discovery — whether your profile appears in other users' feeds

2.4 Location Information

We collect location data in two ways: precise GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) if you grant location permissions, stored directly in your profile; and self-reported country and city entered during onboarding. Location data supports discovery, making connections, and proximity features. If you deny location permissions, you may still use the app manually, though some proximity features may be limited.

2.5 Dating Preferences and Discovery Settings

We collect your dating preferences and discovery filters including minimum and maximum age preferences, distance preferences, and other discovery configuration. We also store whether your profile is hidden from discovery.

2.6 Profile Photos and Media

When you upload profile photos, we collect and store: the image file in secure cloud storage, the storage bucket and file path, whether it is your primary photo, its display position, moderation status (pending, approved, or rejected), quality score and face count from automated photo analysis, whether the photo is blurred pending review, moderation notes and rejection reason, and upload timestamp. Only approved photos are shown in discovery.

2.7 Identity Verification Data

Koneksyon requires identity verification before users can fully access the platform. We collect your selfie image or verification submission, verification status, selfie file path, submission and review timestamps, rejection reason if applicable, and reviewer notes. Verification data may be processed using automated AI systems and human review. Koneksyon does not conduct criminal background checks on users.

2.8 Slides, Likes, and Connection Activity

We record every slide action (like or pass), whether it was a Spark, and the timestamp. We record mutual like events. Daily counters track slides and Sparks used each day, penalty slides, pass streaks, and slide window expiration. Connections are created when two users mutually like each other and include connection vibe, icebreaker prompt, roots connection, language mode, and first message suggestion.

2.9 Message Requests

Before a connection is established, users may send message requests. We collect the sender and recipient identities, the content of the first message, the status of the request (pending, accepted, or declined), and request and response timestamps.

2.10 Messages and Chat Data

We store message text content, sender identity, timestamps, reply-to relationships, original text before edits, edit timestamps, deletion status and scope, moderation notes, and message seen timestamps. Free users may edit within 1 minute of sending. Premium users have 5 minutes. Admin users have no time limit.

2.11 Date Proposals and Safety Acknowledgements

When you send or respond to a date proposal we collect: the associated connection, creator and recipient identities, proposed date, time, timezone, vibe, setting, budget, location mode, invite note, proposal status, expiration timestamp, and all proposal actions with timestamps. Safety acknowledgements are logged separately, recording user identity, connection identity, proposal identity, context, app version, and timestamp.

2.12 Konekt+ and Premium Visibility

When you activate Konekt+, we collect and store: activation and expiration timestamps, duration in minutes, views and likes gained, the source of the activation (purchase, grant, etc.), and your current Konekt+ inventory.

2.13 Credits and Transactions

We maintain a credit system for Konekt+ and Sparks. We collect your current credit balance and a full transaction ledger recording every purchase, spend, refund, and admin grant including credit type, transaction type, amount, balance after transaction, payment provider, provider transaction ID, price in USD, and a reference to the feature that consumed the credit.

2.14 Subscriptions and Entitlements

If you subscribe to Koneksyon Premium, we collect your subscription provider (Apple, Google, or other), subscription status and current period end date, entitlement record confirming premium status, and premium flags on your profile updated in real time.

2.15 Support Requests

When you submit a support request we collect your email address and phone number, the support category, subject line and full message content, any screenshots or attachments, the status, priority, and source of your request, and internal notes, assignment, admin replies, and timestamps of all activity.

2.16 Blocks and Reports

If you block or report another user, we record the identities of both users, the reason and any additional details you provide, timestamps, and the moderation outcome if applicable.

2.17 Notifications

We store in-app notifications including the notification type, title, body text, associated data payload, whether you have read it, and the creation timestamp. Notification types include connection alerts, message notifications, date proposal updates, moderation alerts, and system announcements.

2.18 Terms of Service and Privacy Policy Acceptance

We maintain permanent records of your agreement to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, including your user ID, the exact acceptance timestamp, the policy version you agreed to, the platform you used (iOS or Android), and your IP address if available. These records cannot be deleted even upon account deletion.

2.19 Device and Technical Data

We may collect device type, operating system, app version, IP address, crash logs, diagnostic data, and other technical signals used for security, reliability, fraud prevention, and analytics.

Section 3

How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide and Operate the Service

We use your information to create and maintain your account, display your profile in discovery, power the connection system, enable messaging and date proposals, process Konekt+ and premium features, and deliver all core app functionality.

3.2 To Verify Your Identity

We use verification data to confirm you are a real person, reduce fake profiles and impersonation, prevent fraud, and gate access to full platform features until verification is completed.

3.3 To Power Discovery, Connections, and Recommendations

We use your profile information, cultural background, Caribbean roots, location, preferences, activity, slide history, and verification status to populate your discovery feed and identify potential connections. Discovery results are filtered server-side based on your preferences, our Community Guidelines, and your hidden-from-discovery setting.

3.4 For Safety and Moderation

We use your information to detect scams, spam, fraud, abuse, impersonation, harmful content, and violations of our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines. We may review reports, moderate photos and messages, investigate safety incidents, and take enforcement action including warnings, feature restrictions, suspensions, and permanent bans.

3.5 To Enforce Our Policies and Prevent Re-registration

We use account status data, moderation records, ban records, and the banned identifiers table to enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines, including preventing banned users from creating new accounts using the same email address, phone number, or device identifier.

3.6 For Subscription, Credit, and Billing Management

We use subscription, entitlement, credit balance, and transaction data to manage premium feature access, enforce daily slide and Spark limits for free users, process payments, maintain accurate credit ledgers, and handle billing-related communications and disputes.

3.7 To Deliver Notifications

We use your expo push token and notification preferences to deliver in-app and push notifications, including connection alerts, message notifications, date proposal updates, moderation notices, and system announcements.

3.8 To Improve the Service

We use technical and usage data to analyze how the app is used, fix bugs, improve moderation quality, measure feature performance, and develop new features.

3.9 For Legal and Compliance Purposes

We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests from legal authorities, resolve disputes, investigate policy violations, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect users, Koneksyon, and Zanara Labs LLC from legal liability.

3.10 Legal Basis for EEA and UK Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we process your personal information based on: contractual necessity to provide the Service; legitimate interests including safety and fraud prevention; consent for sensitive personal information, precise location, and identity verification data; and legal obligation where required by applicable law.

Section 4

How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

Your public profile — including your approved photos, first name, age (if enabled), bio, cultural background, Caribbean roots, preferences, and other selected profile fields — may be visible to other users in discovery and on your profile. Messages you send are visible to the recipient. Connection metadata including roots connection and language mode may be visible to your connection.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

Supabase, Inc.

Database, Authentication & Storage

Our primary infrastructure provider based in the United States. All user data including profile information, messages, photos, verification data, and transaction records is stored on Supabase infrastructure under a data processing agreement.

Sightengine

AI Photo Moderation

Used to automatically review and moderate profile photos for content safety. Processes image data on our behalf and is not permitted to retain or use your photos for its own purposes.

RevenueCat

Subscription Management

Manages premium subscriptions and in-app purchases processed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Tracks subscription status, entitlements, and purchase receipts.

Apple App Store & Google Play

Payment Processing

Process in-app purchases and subscriptions directly. Koneksyon receives only a purchase receipt and subscription confirmation — we do not receive or store your payment card details.

MonCash (Digicel Haiti)

Caribbean Payment Processing

Supports mobile payments for Caribbean users. Koneksyon receives a payment confirmation and transaction reference only.

4.4 In Connection With Business Transfers

If Koneksyon or Zanara Labs LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, bankruptcy, or similar transaction, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable privacy law.

4.5 California — Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell personal data in exchange for money. However, certain data sharing with service providers, analytics tools, or technology partners may be characterized as a sale or sharing under California privacy law (CCPA/CPRA). Koneksyon does not share your personal information with third-party advertisers or data brokers for advertising purposes. We do not serve targeted advertising within the app.

Section 5

Data Retention

5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Service, maintain platform safety, manage disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

5.2 After Account Deletion

When you delete your account, your personal information begins the deletion process. Certain information is retained after deletion for legal, safety, and operational reasons including: moderation records, verification data, ban records, blocks and reports, support request records, Terms of Service acceptance records (retained permanently), date safety acknowledgement logs, and payment and billing records where required by law.

5.3 Messages After Account Deletion

Messages you sent to other users may remain visible to those users after you delete your account, unless you deleted those specific messages before account deletion. Deleted messages are flagged as deleted and not displayed, but deletion records may be retained for moderation purposes.

5.4 Formal Deletion and Privacy Requests

Users may submit formal data deletion or export requests under applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA. Such requests are tracked in our systems and reviewed by our team. We may verify your identity before fulfilling a request. Deletion may be denied or limited where retention is required by applicable law, necessary to prevent harm, or necessary to enforce our policies against repeat offenders.

Security

7.1 Security Measures We Use

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information including: encrypted password storage; row-level security (RLS) policies on all database tables; role-based access controls; secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase, Inc.; separation of sensitive data into access-controlled storage buckets; and monitoring and logging of access and activity.

7.2 No Absolute Security Guarantee

No system is completely secure. While we implement strong safeguards, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, cyberattacks, data breaches, or other security incidents will never occur. If a data incident occurs, we will investigate, contain, and mitigate it, and notify affected users or authorities where required by applicable law.

7.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. Do not share your password with anyone. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at privacy@koneksyondatingapp.com.

Section 8

Automated Systems and Artificial Intelligence

8.1 Automated Photo Moderation

Profile photos are processed by automated systems that assess photo quality, count faces, detect inappropriate content, and assign moderation status using AI-assisted image analysis. Human review may also occur for edge cases and appeals.

8.2 Identity Verification AI

Our identity verification process uses automated image comparison technology to compare your selfie submission against your profile photo and assess authenticity. Automated decisions may be subject to human review.

8.3 Discovery and Connection Algorithms

Our discovery system uses automated logic to surface profiles based on your preferences, location, cultural background, Caribbean roots, activity, and other factors. Connection records are enriched with algorithmically generated metadata including roots connection, language mode, icebreaker prompts, and first message suggestions.

8.4 Safety and Abuse Detection

We may use automated systems to detect spam, fraudulent accounts, prohibited conduct, and policy violations. These systems may affect your profile visibility, feature access, or account status. Automated enforcement decisions may be reviewed by human moderators.

8.5 Your Rights Regarding Automated Decisions

Where required by applicable law, including GDPR Article 22, you may have the right to request human review of automated decisions that significantly affect you, including verification decisions and moderation actions. To request human review, contact privacy@koneksyondatingapp.com.
